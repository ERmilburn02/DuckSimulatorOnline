# Privacy Policy

_Last updated Jan 10 2024_

## Orius Games Accounts

By creating and using an Orius Games Account, the following information will be stored:

- Basic Discord Profile details
- Earned Achievements
- Submitted Speedrun times

## The Discord Bot

When interacting inside our Discord Server, our custom-coded bot will store some of your profile details. These include your ID, display name, username, and avatar. These are used to display you on the leaderboard.

### We will never give or sell any information to a third-party.

## TL;DR: We will NEVER share your information!
